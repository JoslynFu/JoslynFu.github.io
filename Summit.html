Business Development
at China-Youth Cloud Summit

We promote the Chinese entrepreneurial spirit and share wisdom from the trailblazers. Our purpose is to provide a platform for young professionals and university students who are interested in the current situation and trends in both Chinese and U.S. Markets.

A total of 380,000 people participated on October 17, 2020.

A total of 770,000 people participated on October 24, 2020.

A total of 38 academic associations and organizations from the United Kingdom and the United States participated in the summit.

Most of the following guests and partner organizations expressed their desire to have a deeper degree of cooperation, including Oxford Vice-Chancellor, Harvard Professor, Oxford Forum, Phoenix, Cultivation, 36kr, and various investment institutions. This has better pave the way for our alliance's follow-up plans.
