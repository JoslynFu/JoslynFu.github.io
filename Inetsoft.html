Digital Analyst
at Inetsoft

InetSoft's solution is foremost a BI app powered by highly flexible data mashup technology. With integrated machine learning capability, data mashup further enables human designed dashboards and reports to be mashed up with machine generated intelligence.

I practiced my project management and market research skills by contributing to the Cloud Data Sources project. During the project, I researched potential metrics and KPIs for various dashboard companies, provided clear definitions of each, and determined realistic range of data. Furthermore, I helped with dashboard building simulation by creating data in basic table spreadsheet form or manually entered data for each datasource. Through this project, I learned how to conduct competitive product analysis in an organized and analytical structure. I have also grown strong interest in data mining and applying statistical modelling to real-world data science problems.

Moreover, I proactively initiated the Lead Lost Reason project with the intention of figuring out the reasons why some people ended up not choosing our product. For this project, I created detailed timeline to draft survey questions, choose the most suitable survey tool, and strategically send out survey at a given time of a day. I successfully collected useful information such as the fact that a majority of the respondents considered Looker, one of our competitors, as another option, and people are looking for time difference impacts support. My experience with this project made me realize the importance and the necessity to have analytic abilities and innovative spirit in a corporate world.
