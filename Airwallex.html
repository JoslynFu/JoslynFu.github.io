Product Management Intern
at Airwallex

Airwallex is a global payments fintech transforming the way businesses move and manage money domestically and internationally. It is a unicorn backed by Sequoia Capital, Hillhouse, DST Global, Tencent, Alibaba Entrepreneurs Fund, Salesforce Venture, and Mastercard.

I worked as a product management intern at Airwallex over the summer 2021. My main work involves three projects as below:

1. Baidu oCPC API Integration Project

By feeding conversion data back to Baidu oCPC model, we aim to reduce the ratio of signups that could not be reached by the registered phone number to industry benchmark and improve overall conversion rate of Baidu signups. Thus, I led the execution stage of this conversion data feeding project on search engine advertisements, which is estimated to save $200k for the marketing team yearly.

- oCPC model: Optimized Cost per Click (oCPC) model is an AI model that estimates the conversion rate of each click in real-time and sets a bidding price based on the competition. It studies user behavior in the media (e.g. Baidu) and strengthens ads exposure to users that have a higher probability of conversion to the desired behavior.

2. Get Paid Product

Get Paid is a simple link that allows merchants to get paid fast. Get Paid leverages the payment acceptance core capabilities and allows SMEs to receive foreign currency payments to their Global Accounts to avoid FX fees.

I defined success metrics, implemented google analytics tracking, and explored post MVP plans such as integration with other invoicing softwares and adding self-onboarding to achieve an easier checkout experience.

3. Membership

Building on our existing membership plan, I collaborated with strategy analysts to refine user experience, such as fixing bugs in our internal pricing system, expanding membership benefits, and interviewing customers for their business needs.
